Just a quick heads-up -- indie strategy tactical RPG "Those Who Rule" just went live on Steam today. I've been following it through its development for a few years now, and it's exciting to finally to be able to play it past the first few chapters!

I'll talk about it more once I start playing it through, but if you're at all interested, first day sales are the best sales, and it's even at a discount for a few days.
