In the tradition of such classics as Final Fantasy VII, "Nine Lives Chronicles" follow a young woman named Claire as she discovers that she has nine lives. Her epic story will span multiple lifetimes, from a lifetime in the distant past of a war-torn world, to a lifetime in the future of a world on the brink of destruction. In each lifetime, Claire will meet different people who also have nine lives, and together they must use their extra lives to help others and make the world a better place.

Depending on questions answered during the opening scene, Claire will first discover her extra lives when she:

- is mortally wounded in battle, but inexplicably wakes up unharmed.- is attacked by a vicious monster, but is saved by a mysterious stranger who also has nine lives.- witnesses the death of a loved one, but is given the chance to save them by using one of her extra lives.

Throughout her journey, Claire will use her nine lives to:

- save people from danger- help others achieve their goals- change the course of history

With each life she lives, Claire will come one step closer to understanding the true purpose of her extra lives.

"The Nine-Lives Chronicles" has (as you'd expect) nine different endings. In one ending, Claire uses all of her lives to save others and dies contentedly knowing that she made a difference in the world. In another ending, Claire discovers that her extra lives are a curse, and she spends her remaining days trying to find a way to rid herself of them. There are also several endings in which Claire fails to make a difference in the world, or only makes things worse. It's up to you to decide which ending Claire will ultimately achieve.

Since this is a turn-based RPG, gameplay consists mostly of Claire exploring her world, talking to people, and engaging in battles. Battles are fought using a traditional turn-based system, with Claire and her party members taking turns attacking and defending. However, since Claire has nine lives, she can choose to use one of her extra lives to continue fighting even if she is defeated. This adds an extra layer of strategy to battles, as you must decide when to use Claire's extra lives wisely.
