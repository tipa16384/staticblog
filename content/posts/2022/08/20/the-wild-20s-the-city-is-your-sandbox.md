**Number**: 20. **Genre**: Open-world sandbox game. **Title**: "The Wild 20s". **Outline**: In this open-world sandbox game, you play as a young adult in the 1920s, exploring the city and trying to make your way in the world. You can choose to be honest or dishonest, work hard or party hard, find love or get into trouble. It's up to you!

After an opening cutscene, you find yourself in your apartment on the outskirts of the city.

You can explore the different rooms and get to know your neighbours. As you explore the city, you'll come across different opportunities and challenges. You can choose to take them on or ignore them, but be warned that some choices will have consequences. 

Every location in the city can be explored, and there are plenty of side quests and activities to keep you busy. You can also choose to get involved in the city's underground scene, if you're feeling adventurous. 

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new abilities and items, which will help you in your quest to survive and thrive in the city. Choose a life of crime, and you'll have to be careful not to get caught by the police. Or, you could start your own business and become a success in the city. Or just spend the nights partying and enjoying the city's nightlife. 

Once you have earned enough experience in your chosen profession, you can begin to make a name for yourself in the city. 

While "The Wild 20s" isn't a multiplayer game, you can still interact with other players online. You can share tips and tricks, or just chat about your experiences in the game. Among the professions you can unlock are entrepreneur, socialite, musician, artist, and more.

You'll rub shoulders with the celebrities of the age, like F. Scott Fitzgerald and Zelda Fitzgerald. And you'll have to watch out for the hazards of the city, like gangs, corruption, and disease. 

Your starting apartment is in the Outer Slums neighborhood, a dangerous place for someone alone. You must be careful not to attract too much attention, or you'll find yourself in a fight you can't win. 

If you're feeling brave, you can explore the city's seedy underside, where you'll find illegal gambling dens, underground boxing clubs, and more. But be warned, it's easy to get caught up in the criminal lifestyle and end up in jail – or worse. 

As you make your way through the game, you'll level up and earn new skills and items. You can also purchase upgrades for your apartment, which will give you access to new areas of the city. Each new neighborhood brings with it new challenges and opportunities. 

You can always find new quests at the newspaper stands, but be warned – some of them are more dangerous than others. The best opportunities, however, are usually found off the beaten path. 

"The Wild 20s" is similar to the "Far Cry" and "Assassin's Creed" games in that it is an open-world sandbox game with a focus on exploration and freedom. However, "The Wild 20s" has a more light-hearted tone and focuses on adventure and opportunity, rather than violence and stealth. You may still find yourself climbing radio towers and using zip-lines to get around the city, but you'll also be dancing the Charleston, sipping cocktails, and attending raucous parties. 

"The Wild 20s" is a game for those who want to experience the Roaring Twenties firsthand. It's a time of social change, economic prosperity, and cultural flamboyance. It's also a time of danger, with gangs, corruption, and disease lurking around every corner. But if you're feeling brave, "The Wild 20s" is the game for you.

**Graphics**

"The Wild 20s" has a colorful, cartoony art style that brings the Roaring Twenties to life. The city is detailed and full of life, and the different neighborhoods each have their own unique flavor.

**Gameplay**

"The Wild 20s" is an open-world sandbox game that gives you the freedom to choose your own adventure. You can explore the city at your own pace, and there are plenty of side quests and activities to keep you busy. The game also features a day/night cycle, which affects the type of people and events you'll encounter in the city.

**Controls**

"The Wild 20s" uses a standard third-person control scheme. You'll use the left analog stick to move your character, and the right analog stick to control the camera. The face buttons are used for various actions, such as jumping, interacting with objects, and using items.

"The Wild 20s" is a fun and unique open-world sandbox game that captures the spirit of the Roaring Twenties. The freedom to choose your own path, combined with the colorful art style and detailed city, makes "The Wild 20s" a game worth checking out.
