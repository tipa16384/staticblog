I asked OpenAI's GPT-3 to generate 31 different videogame ideas, one for each day of Blaugust, each dealing in some way with that particular day of the month. Here's its idea for August 1st.

"One: The Game" is an online multiplayer game where you play as one of a group of super-powered beings who must work together to stop a madman from taking over the world.

The game is played in a third-person perspective, and you can choose to play as one of several different characters, each with their own unique abilities.

You'll need to use your powers to defeat enemies and solve puzzles as you make your way through the game's levels. The game's characters have an unusual collection of powers, including the ability to fly, super strength, and the ability to control the elements. You'll need to use these powers to defeat enemies, solve puzzles, and progress through the game's levels.

The game is designed to be played with friends, so you'll need to work together in order to succeed. And if you don't have friends, the game can be played solo with the help of AI companions.

The developers of the game promise to deliver additional live content that will allow you to name and raise a pet, as well as take on new challenges. Once the pet becomes an adult, it can be used in battle to help you defeat enemies.

Unlike other games in the superhero genre, "One: The Game" does not feature combat between players. Instead, the focus is on working together to complete the game's objectives. Those objectives include rescuing hostages, disarming bombs, and defeating the game's final boss, who, in a dramatic twist, turns out to be the player's own pet.

The game is a sequel of sorts to the Super Nintendo's single player action game, "One: Kagayake! Kuruneko", which is remembered in the present day by its groundbreaking use of cel-shaded graphics.
