Haven't beaten this one yet. The five Fiery Hounds don't do much damage and one tank (this is my other tank now) can easily hold them with the standard two clerics keeping him up, and the druid healing the raid.

Burninator was the only one of my wizards who showed up at raid time, so he got a spot, but in his typical AFK-ness, refused to stop nuking the wrong targets. Similarly, Magetaur kept doing AE nukes even though I told her to hold off until they were nearly all dead, since you have to kill them all within a few rounds or they self-rez.

When I try this again, I'm going to bring only melee DPS. They don't have long, non-interruptable  spells.
