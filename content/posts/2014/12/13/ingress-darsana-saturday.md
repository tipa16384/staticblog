It's #Darsana today! Ingress players from all over the northeast are gathering today in Hartford for the Darsana anomaly. I don't know the storyline -- I haven't played much Ingress since work transferred me to a location without many nearby portals -- but there was no way I was going to miss this event in Hartford. And besides, end of next week, I'm transferred back to Hartford so Ingress will be back on for me in 2015.

It was super fun biking all over the county capturing portals. It could take HOURS to get home, with all the detours. VERY excited about being able to do that again. Especially since I will no longer have the 18 mile commute to work at current location to keep in shape.
