Spoiler alert: They're in China.

Last week, game developer Todd Walsh and publisher Mike Gnade held a live preview of their new game expansion, "Set a Watch: Swords of the Coin". During the Q&A session afterward, [they expanded on how difficult it is](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1143664373) to print games with lower print runs or custom components in the US or the EU. So, everyone goes to China. But with world shipping in upheaval, getting the games *from* China... that's an issue. Rock Manor Games had to do a Go Fund Me (it was some other fundraising site, but same diff) to raise money to get the games out of China.

So let's find my Kickstarter games.

[![](https://chasingdings.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/image-7-1024x510.png)](https://chasingdings.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/image-7.png)Playing Cats

Starting off with a counter-example: Playing Cats advertises right on top -- and in its video -- that it's made in the US. Their previous Kickstarter was going to be produced in Taiwan.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/034/426/680/cdf8f4131aa90c0b2e75d6f9af8484c9_original.jpeg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&w=700&fit=max&v=1628155853&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=eb9e3be0a3ec7daeb1ce7f226cf0726d)Zombie Life Insurance

Zombie Life Insurance is delayed two weeks, possibly more as of last update. Well, that's not too bad.

![A quick playing cooperative dungeon crawler for 1 to 4 players, featuring a modular dungeon, epic boss battles and detailed miniatures!](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/033/980/717/8443d0a4f053a5116bf1d591988a3f0a_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&w=700&fit=max&v=1624423159&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=073d9725fe44a53f7be8e797f2b11956)Tiny Epic Dungeons

Somewhat concerned about the shipping, but they built in a buffer and they hope they won't get further delayed.

![Three new series of collected comics by Ctrl+Alt+Del.](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/032/107/237/5439d53cd317949426a2a4a65cf1c29b_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&crop=faces&w=1552&h=873&fit=crop&v=1611416308&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=009ada86349ab7602024895087117f3b)Ctrl-Alt-Del collection

On a boat at the moment. Things are looking good for the US. Books stuck in a port in Australia. UK and EU are on boats.

![If you missed the campaign, click the blue ](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/031/082/656/29aa5ef8fda0770ea54c37d1b85e13ed_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&crop=faces&w=1552&h=873&fit=crop&v=1603201848&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=8f0ae42f8863321312da120f28f7aea3)Lords of the Chords

Slipped to December or January, depending on how you do the math. Was originally scheduled for July. I don't think most of the delay was due to shipping, though. I don't know if this is coming from China or Singapore.

![Heroes can’t go it alone-every Hero needs a sidekick! Tokyo Sidekick is a cooperative board game for 1-4 players from Japanime Games.](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/029/965/879/d35b42eba0e7b4c87a2b23c08a40ae7f_original.png?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&crop=faces&w=1552&h=873&fit=crop&v=1595862388&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=fe92bd5c480fbc2a594de893a17f23ae)Tokyo Sidekick

The publisher just has too many irons in the fire. This is over a year late and still no date on when it will get here. I've stopped backing Japanime Games projects.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/034/857/894/47ecf1675b7a94a705e1db6acaaa81bc_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&w=700&fit=max&v=1631538009&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=34987bdca9322fc7d99f510386bdc4d9)Tales from the Loop

Game's done, they just don't know when or if they will be able to get it on a boat.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/034/197/948/d00b69ef3d39e2c05f77dbefe6c6e2c6_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&w=700&fit=max&v=1626208459&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=de9bd35f5e0c0ee6848c818978ee3ec8)Mystery Dice

Their shipping costs have tripled. They are over a year late on this one. I was hoping for Christmas LAST year, then various birthdays. Christmas THIS year seems unlikely. Maybe next Christmas.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/034/159/661/75b9a38188a0a322fdaf4cdbc4406776_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&w=700&fit=max&v=1625868852&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=ba41faff08749c496ed57979948bfc3c)Frosthaven

Concerned about shipping, but still committed to getting the game in our hands toward the start of 2022. Should just be a year late, though it's unlikely related to shipping.

![A complete redesign of the original game of autoduelling. Car Wars Sixth Edition plays faster and includes new plastic miniatures.](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/027/129/935/bf24270c1b0cfb31a97684646fe08870_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&crop=faces&w=1552&h=873&fit=crop&v=1573125874&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=ea4dc929656c971a898fc9889d73254e)Car Wars, Sixth Edition

This is a year late (again, not entirely due to shipping issues). All the containers are now landed stateside, so we should be introducing this to Game Night pretty soon.

![](https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/011/599/077/8e2f9e3c95486301d701a5e8a8bfd7f4_original.jpg?ixlib=rb-4.0.2&crop=faces&w=1552&h=873&fit=crop&v=1463685045&auto=format&frame=1&q=92&s=b4c5796cb3228b00fae1d3f9761b8c22)Star Trek Axanar
