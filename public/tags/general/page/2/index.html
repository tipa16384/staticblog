Welcome to your New Week

These are the Days of the Week I’ve been playing around with new names for the days of the week for a few months now. I don’t understand why we have to honor the Norse pantheon in this way, or the arbitrary homage to the Sun and the Moon. When I was taking Japanese, I was struck by how similar our day names were to theirs. 日曜日 – nichiyoubi – Sun Day. 月曜日 – getsuyoubi – Moon Day. It wasn’t a coincidence. Japan adopted its version of the Western calendar during the Meiji Restoration, as it adopted so many Western customs.
